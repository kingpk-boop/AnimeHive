The Eminence in Shadow

Kage no Jitsuryokusha ni Naritakute!

For as long as he can remember, Minoru Kagenou has been fixated on becoming as strong as possible, leading him to undertake all kinds of rigorous training. However, his goal is not to be recognized by others but to blend into the crowd. While pretending to be an average student by day, he fights criminals at night.

When a tragic accident seems to end his ambitions, Minoru awakens in another world where magic exists. Seizing the opportunity, he takes on the moniker "Shadow" and establishes Shadow Garden, an organization dedicated to combating a mysterious cult.
